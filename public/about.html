About Me

In January 2023, I made a life changing decision to pursue a dream I had nurtured for 2 years to become an expert in any aspect of computer technology. I enrolled to be trained as a full-stack software engineer at Alx Africa, a training I embarked on for 12 months.

I branched into software engineering from a background in medicine, having been working as a licensed veterinary medical doctor in Lagos, Nigeria.

My drive to delve into software engineering was drawn from a dream to provide novel solutions to medical problems using technology, and to provide indigenous technological solutions to African problems so as to improve the quality of life in Africa.

My key areas of focus are the health, agro, finance, and education tech sectors
